    Hi there! I'm Jinshui (pronounciated jeen-shway), though many people also know me as Jimmy. My journey began in Jining, China, and has taken me through the cities of Tianjin and Xi'an before leading me here to Durham, NC.
    Currently, I am a PhD student at Duke University in Brain Stimulation Engineering Lab. Under the guidance of Prof. Stefan M Goetz, I get to explore how multi-cell power electronics can drive innovation in clean energy, green transportation, and even neuroscience! You can have a preview of my work here in my cv.
    I'm always excited to connect with new people, whether it's for a creative project or a new friendship. Please feel free to email me at jinshui.zhang@duke.edu.
